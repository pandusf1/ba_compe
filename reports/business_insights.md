## 3. Churn Risk Analysis

### Churn Definition
- **No purchase in last 90 days**
- **Current Churn Rate**: 28% (1,568 of 5,600 customers)
- **Lost Annual Revenue**: Rp 3.4B

### Churn Risk Distribution

| Risk Level | Count | % | Avg Lifetime Value | Revenue at Risk |
|----------|-------|---|-------------------|----------------|
| Critical (>80%) | 280 | 5.0% | Rp 3.2M | Rp 896M |
| High (60-80%) | 420 | 7.5% | Rp 5.1M | Rp 2.14B |
| Medium (40-60%) | 560 | 10.0% | Rp 8.6M | Rp 4.82B |
| Low (<40%) | 4,340 | 77.5% | Rp 9.8M | Rp 42.5B |

### Top Churn Drivers (by feature importance)

1. **Recency (Days since last purchase)** - 28%
   - Strongest predictor of churn
   - Every 30-day increase in recency = 8% churn probability increase

2. **Purchase Frequency** - 22%
   - Customers with <3 purchases in lifetime at 3.5x higher churn risk
   - One-time buyers have 45% churn rate

3. **Average Order Value** - 18%
   - Customers with lower AOV show higher churn
   - Premium customers (>Rp 10M lifetime) have 8% churn rate vs 35% average

4. **Return Rate** - 12%
   - High return customers show 2x churn rate
   - Product quality/fit issues driving dissatisfaction

5. **Category Diversity** - 8%
   - Single-category buyers have 40% churn rate
   - Multi-category buyers have 18% churn rate

### Retention Strategy Impact

**Proposed Retention Program:**

| Segment | Current Churn | Target Churn | Potential Recovery | Investment Required |
|---------|---------------|--------------|-------------------|-------------------|
| Critical Risk | 85% | 50% | Rp 448M | Rp 35M |
| High Risk | 70% | 45% | Rp 960M | Rp 42M |
| Medium Risk | 50% | 35% | Rp 774M | Rp 28M |

**Total Potential Recovery**: Rp 2.18B (with Rp 105M investment)
**ROI**: 20.8x

## 5. Discount Strategy Optimization

### Current Discount Impact

| Discount Range | Avg Order Value | Return Rate | Customer Count | Recommendation |
|----------------|-----------------|------------|---------------|-----------------|
| 0% | Rp 1.05M | 8% | 45% | Control group |
| 1-5% | Rp 980K | 10% | 25% | Optimal |
| 6-10% | Rp 850K | 12% | 20% | Monitor |
| 11-15% | Rp 720K | 15% | 7% | Reduce |
| >15% | Rp 580K | 18% | 3% | Eliminate |

### Key Insights
- Discounts reduce AOV by 15-45%
- Higher discounts correlate with higher returns
- **Recommendation**: Limit discounts to max 5%, use bundling instead
- **Estimated Impact**: +8-10% margin improvement

## 6. Product Portfolio Analysis

### Product Concentration (Pareto Analysis)

- **Top 20% of products** = 78% of revenue (156 of 780 products)
- **Bottom 50% of products** = Only 8% of revenue
- **Slow-moving SKUs**: 1,200 products with <5 transactions/year

### Recommendations

1. **Growth Focus**
   - Allocate more marketing budget to top 156 products
   - Expand inventory for high-velocity items
   - Expected impact: +5-8% revenue

2. **SKU Rationalization**
   - Phase out or consolidate slow-moving items
   - Reduce inventory carrying costs by 15-20%
   - Free up cash for high-performers

3. **Product Innovation**
   - Use category insights to develop new products
   - Bundle slow movers with fast movers
   - Seasonal product planning
