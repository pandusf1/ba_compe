## 🎯 DELIVERY TIPS

✅ **Pacing**: 20-22 min slides + 3-5 min Q&A  
✅ **Tone**: Confident, conversational, data-driven  
✅ **Energy**: Moderate-to-high; avoid monotone  
✅ **Eye Contact**: Look at audience, not slides  
✅ **Pauses**: Strategic pauses after key insights  
✅ **Emphasis**: Hand gestures on ROI and metrics  
✅ **Transitions**: "Let's drill in..." / "This leads us to..."

---

## 🎨 VISUAL CONSISTENCY

- **Colors**: Professional blues, greens, oranges
- **Fonts**: Sans-serif (Arial, Calibri)
- **Labels**: Always include units (Rp, %, days)
- **Charts**: Consistent style throughout
- **Clutter**: Minimal text - visuals tell story
