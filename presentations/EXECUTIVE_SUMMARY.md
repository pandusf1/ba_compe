## 🔍 KEY FINDINGS

### 1️⃣ Churn Crisis
- **28% of customers inactive** in last 90 days
- **Lost Revenue**: Rp 3.4B annually
- **Root Cause**: Low engagement; poor retention programs
- **Opportunity**: Recover Rp 2.18B (20.8x ROI)

### 2️⃣ Cross-Sell Gap
- **56% of customers** purchase from single category
- **3,200 customers** with cross-sell potential
- **Untapped Value**: Rp 8.96B
- **Opportunity**: Capture Rp 1.3-2.2B

### 3️⃣ Discount Inefficiency
- **Deep discounts reduce AOV by 15-45%**
- **Current Impact**: -Rp 800M in margin
- **Solution**: Cap discounts at 5%, shift to bundling
- **Opportunity**: +Rp 800M margin improvement

### 4️⃣ Regional Imbalance
- **Jakarta dominates** with 40% of revenue
- **Tier-2 cities underpenetrated**
- **Expansion Potential**: +Rp 1.4B (Year 2)

### 5️⃣ Revenue Concentration
- **Top 20% of products = 78% of revenue**
- **Portfolio Risk**: High dependency on few SKUs
- **Growth Opportunity**: +5-8% through diversification
